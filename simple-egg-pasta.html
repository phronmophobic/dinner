Simple Egg Pasta

AKA Pasta al uovo, the most simple pasta recipe that can be used to make all kinds of noodles or filled pasta shapes. Can be scaled to the number of people by just multiplying the recipe. This recipe is for one serving.

Ingredients

Recipe

  1. Pour the flour out onto a clean surface and form it into a mound with a hole in the middle, kind of like a little volcano

  2. Crack egg(s) into the middle of the mound

  3. Using a fork or your fingers, slowly integrate flour from the edge of the mount into the middle and mix with the flour, until a dough forms

  4. If dough is too dry, add a bit of water. If dough is too wet, add a bit more flour

  5. Knead for several minutes until flour and egg are well incorporated and dough is fairly firm and uniform in color

  6. Cover with plastic and let sit for at least 30 minutes in a cool place before rolling out into whatever shape you want
