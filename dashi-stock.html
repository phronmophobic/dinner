Dashi Stock

The most essential Japanese cooking stock that becomes the base of many other sauces and soups.

Ingredients

Recipe

  1. Place konbu and water in a sauce pan or stock pot over high heat

  2. Keep an eye on the pot and as soon as you start to see the water come to a low simmer, remove from the heat

  3. Add katsuobushi to the water, set a timer for five minutes

  4. When the five minutes are up, strain the liquid into another vessel and discard the used katsuobushi and konbu. If you want to, you can use these same ingredients to make a second batch, much like you might brew a second batch of tea with the same leaves. It won't be as strong but will still have really great flavor

  5. Store in your fridge/freezer in deli containers or use immediately to make miso soup
