Miso Soup

Ingredients

Recipe

  1. Place dashi stock in a sauce pan or stock pot, making sure there's enough room for all the liquid plus the extra stuff you plan to put in the soup, set heat to high to bring up to a boil

  2. When the dashi is boiling, lower heat and keep at a simmer

  3. Drain the tofu and pad away excess moisture from the block

  4. Cut the tofu into 3/4 inch to 1 inch cubes

  5. Cut scallions into whatever sized slices you like

  6. Cut shiitake mushrooms in half (or smaller pieces if you'd like)

  7. Throw everything in the pot with the dashi, raise the heat a bit and bring everything up to a boil. Stir until the miso paste has dissolved into the dashi
